	Sales Performance Dashboard – Objective

•	Provides a clear, interactive, and consolidated view of overall sales performance, profitability, and product trends
•	Enables analysis across multiple dimensions such as year, city, customer, and product line
•	Helps stakeholders quickly understand financial and operational performance
•	Supports data-driven decision-making

	Highlights key KPIs including:
o	Total Sales
o	Profit Margin (%)
o	Profit Margin Value
o	Top-Selling Product
o	Sales Achievement vs Target
•	Displays KPIs at the top for instant assessment of business health and goal progress
•	Allows year-wise comparison of sales quantity trends (2003, 2004, and 2005) to identify growth or decline

	Focuses on product-level insights using:
o	Pie charts
o	Tree maps
o	Bar charts
•	Identifies top-performing and bottom-performing products
•	Shows category-wise contribution to total sales and quantity
•	Helps optimize inventory planning, pricing strategies, and marketing decisions

	Provides interactive slicers for:
o	Year
o	City
o	Customer Name
o	Product Line
•	Enables drill-down analysis to uncover hidden insights
•	Transforms raw data into meaningful, actionable insights for better sales monitoring, profitability analysis, and strategic planning
